AdMob: app-ads.txt

This page hosts the app-ads.txt content for AdMob/Ad exchange verification. Uploading the file to the root of your domain helps authorized sellers display ads for your app.

What is app-ads.txt?

An app-ads.txt is a plain text file that indicates which ad networks may sell ads for your apps. Google AdMob recommends you host app-ads.txt at the root of your developer domain.
